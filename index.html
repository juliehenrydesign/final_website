Meet Our Head Bakers....

John and Mary have been providing award winning recipes for over 20 years. Their combined culinary expertise has landed them on multiple baking competitions on the Food Network. Here at the Balery, they use only the finest ingredients imported from all over the world. We employ over 12 bakers and can handle a venue of any size or theme.

Mary, mother of two little girls, says she gets most of her inspiration from her time in the kitchen with her girls. She has been the head Chef at many of New York's finest restaurants. Not only has she attended some of the most prestigious culinary schools in Europe as well as the states, she also has aquired an MBA from Columbia University.

John, father of three, lives on Long Island. He has been baking since childhood. He hails from Chicago and has won numerous awards for his "healthy" baking techniques. He has won the James Beard award and loves to bake with his kids. He attended NY culinary schools and has travelled extensively throughout Europe to find the next, best, flavor.
